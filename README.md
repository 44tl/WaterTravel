## Study: Water Travel & Transmedium UAPs

### 1. Definition of Transmedium Travel

**Transmedium** refers to the ability of an object to seamlessly transition between two different physical media without loss of speed, control, or structural integrity.

Common examples of transmedium travel:

- **Air to water** (e.g., sky to ocean)
    
- **Water to air** (e.g., ocean to sky)
    
- **Air to space** (atmosphere to vacuum)
    
- **Water to space** (direct transition without surfacing)
    

Conventional human vehicles cannot truly operate transmedium. Submarines cannot fly. Aircraft cannot submerge. Rockets cannot enter water at high speeds.

### 3. Water Entry Physics – The Problem

Human-made objects entering water at high speed experience **catastrophic deceleration** due to water's density (≈800× denser than air).

|Speed|Human Object Outcome|
|---|---|
|30 mph|Diving (controlled, low impact)|
|150 mph|Water acts like concrete|
|Mach 1+|Instant disintegration|

**UAP observations contradict this:**

- Objects enter water at hundreds of mph without slowing
    
- No sonic boom or cavitation signature
    
- No heat damage upon exit
    
- Sometimes emerge completely dry
    

---

### 4. Hypotheses for Transmedium Capability

#### A. Cavitation Manipulation

The UAP may generate a **supercavitation bubble** a pocket of gas around the object that reduces friction by 80%+.  
_Problem_: Human supercavitating torpedoes (e.g., Russian Shkval) can only go 200-300 mph, cannot exit water, and create massive noise & bubble trails.

#### B. Density Reduction Field

The UAP may emit a field that temporarily **lowers the density of water** around it, making water behave like thin air.  
This would explain smooth transitions and dry exits.

#### C. Phased / Interdimensional Transition

Some researchers propose the UAP does not physically "swim" through water but **shifts between dimensions** where water's physical properties do not fully apply.  
This aligns with portal/timeline theories but remains outside mainstream physics.

#### D. Gravity Wave Manipulation

If the UAP generates its own gravitational field, it can:

- Repel water molecules before contact
    
- Create a vacuum tunnel through any medium (air, water, rock)
    
- Transition without deceleration

### 6. Why Water Travel Matters

If transmedium UAPs are real, the implications are profound:

- **Global oceans become operational domains** for non-human intelligence 70% of Earth's surface largely unmonitored underwater.
    
- **Submarine detection** may be obsolete if UAP can move silently between air and sea.
    
- **Undersea bases** are a plausible hypothesis for UAP staging areas, hidden from satellite and most radar.
    
- **Water entry/exit without signature** suggests propulsion not based on combustion, propellers, or known hydrodynamics.
    

---

### 7. Official Recognition

In recent years, transmedium UAPs have entered legitimate discussion:

- **2021 ODNI Report** (Office of the Director of National Intelligence) explicitly mentioned UAPs demonstrating "transmedium travel" as a key characteristic requiring investigation.
    
- **NASA's UAP Study (2023)** acknowledged transmedium objects as a priority for sensor calibration.
    
- **AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office)** currently investigates transmedium cases with Navy sonar, radar, and satellite data.
